About Me

I am a detail-oriented developer with knowledge and skills to build full stack web applications. I fell in love with technology as a classroom teacher and how it connects humans globally. My passion for teaching math and problem solving led me to my bootcamp journey with Georgia Tech to become certified as a full stack developer. Frontend design immediately captivated my creative side, while the logic of backend intrigued my analytical side. I began looking at websites from a whole new perspective.

Teaching made me acutely aware of how easily the brain becomes distracted. My development process when building an application is to design clean, user-friendly layouts that are engaging and brand focused. My goal is to create an attention-grabbing message that aligns with the brand's vision and core values. Enjoy viewing my work samples, and please contact me if you are interested in colloborating together on your next project.
